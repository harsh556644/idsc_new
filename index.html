About Us

One Council, Three Disciplines

IDSC brings together designers, engineers and founders-in-training under one roof. We run the workshops, mentorship circles and project sprints that turn a student with an idea into a builder with a portfolio.

🚀

Innovation

We push members to question defaults and prototype fast, with real feedback loops from people who've shipped before.

💡

Creativity

Design thinking isn't a buzzword here — it's the lens we use to turn vague problems into clear, buildable solutions.

🤝

Collaboration

Cross-disciplinary teams of designers, developers and strategists working shoulder to shoulder on every project.

📈

Entrepreneurship

From idea validation to pitch decks, we equip members with the practical playbook real founders use.
