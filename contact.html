FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join IDSC? +
Any current student is welcome to apply, regardless of major or experience level. We look for curiosity over credentials.
Is membership free? +
Yes. Membership is free for all enrolled students. Some specialized workshops may have limited seats.
Do I need prior experience to join a project team? +
No. Most members start by attending workshops and join project teams once they find a vertical that fits their interests.
How do I propose a workshop or event? +
Send us a message through this contact form or the community chat with your idea, and a vertical lead will follow up.
